FRUITS

Fruits are the sweet, fleshy, edible part of a plant that usually contains seeds. They are a rich source of vitamins, minerals, and fiber, and are an important part of a balanced diet. Fruits can be eaten raw, frozen, canned, or dried, and can be used in many ways, including in jams and jellies, desserts, and main dishes.
